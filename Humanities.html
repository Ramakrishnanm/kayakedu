Humanities, Liberal Arts & Social Sciences

If you are interested in studying and experimenting with music, literature, and languages, you should consider opting for the humanities as a college degree. This course includes the study of languages, literature, art, music, philosophy, history, law, politics and religion. Scholars in the humanities are said as "humanity scholars" or humanists. Humanities, Liberal arts, and social science are almost the same, which deals with different and overlapping categories in studying a human in his natural habitat. These have been a significant field of study since the times of Ancient Greece, and the knowledge was used to explore poetry, history, culture, and the arts. In broader terms, students earning a liberal arts degree help them to formulate effective arguments, and solve problems. And the degree in Social science helps the students to shape their reasoning, critical thinking as well analytical skills. Their careers include opportunities in psychology, sociology, economics and political science.

HSEE

The Humanities and Social Sciences Entrance Examination or HSEE is a national level entrance test conducted every year by Indian Institute of Technology Madras for admission to the master programme offered by the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS) of the institute.

Historian

Historians are academicians and researchers, whose field of expertise is to study events of the past by obtaining historical data from libraries, archives, artifacts, and determining the authenticity of historical data. They are concerned with the continuous, methodical narrative and research, of past events of all history in time. These experts work in museums, archives, historical societies, and research organizations.

Public Affairs and Policy Expert


Public affairs work includes policy functions, which is to create and maintain a favorable public image, manage media releases, and develop social media programs to manipulate the public perception of their organization. These experts design policies that influence local, national and international levels of favorable efficacy for their organization. 

Political Science

Political science is a branch of social science that deals with theory, description, analysis and prediction of political behavior, systems and politics. These scientists study politics and its power from domestic, and international perspectives. Typically these experts get employed in administrative roles, a think tank (research organization), a political lobby group, university or a non-profit organization.
