Work Rights & Stay Back

*  240 days part time or 120 days full time in a year .

*  18 months Residence permit

*  Authorization to work 20 hours per week

Documents Required

*  Details of your passport and recent photograph

*  Copy of letter of acceptance for studies

*  Proof of Funds – Applicants must demonstrate that they have sufficient liquid assets to pay the tuition fees, living expenses and travelling cost without engaging in employment in Germany

* Proof of valid private or public health insurance

* Your tenancy agreement, if applicable

* For English-taught courses, you’ll need to provide a TOEFL or IELTS score

* Proof of financial resources

* Certificates of past academic work and achievements

Admission  Eligibility 

Each university has their own requirements for GPAs and German language & IELTS language requirements. However, generally 60% or above are considerable.

Bachelors & Post graduate diploma: 10+2 with minimum 50%. German language A1/A2 level score is required & IELTS 6.0 overall not less than 5.5 in each modules

Masters: 10+2+3 or 10+2+4. Some University may ask for IELTS score of 6 or 6.5 overall.
