Engineering

Engineering is a stream of college education that covers diverse areas like machines, building structures, software, and systems, with each having its own specialization. It involves the application of science, technology, and math to design and develop innovative solutions to everyday problems and for the betterment of our lives.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is an area of study that combines mathematical principles and engineering physics with material sciences to design, analyse, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.

Civil Engineering

Being one of the broadest and oldest engineering disciplines, Civil Engineering extends across many technical specialties. It is the application of physical and scientific principles for solving the problems of the society. 

Aeronautical Engineering

Aeronautical Engineering is an undergraduate programme that deals primarily with aviation and its related systems. It deals with the study, design, and manufacture of operational aircraft within the Earth’s atmosphere. 

Chemical Engineering

Chemical Engineering is a multi-disciplinary branch that combines elements of natural and experimental sciences, life science, mathematics, and economics to design and maintain chemical plants and the development of chemical processes for converting raw materials or chemicals into valuable products. 

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) is an integrated branch of engineering that deals with the technological aspects of power generation, the design and application of circuitry, and other practical applications of electricity. 

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Electronics and Communication Engineering deals with the research, design, testing, and development of electronic devices, circuits, communication equipment like transmitter, receiver, and integrated circuits. It opens up a world of opportunities both professionally and academically.

Mechatronic Engineering

Mechatronic Engineering, or simply Mechatronics is an interdisciplinary stream of engineering that is at the intersection of mechanics, electronics, and computing. It includes a combination of robotics, electronics, computer, telecommunications, systems, control, and product engineering and prepares students to create simpler, smarter systems.
