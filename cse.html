Technical Writer

A Technical Writer is a professional writer who communicates complex information. They create technical documentation that includes things like instruction manuals,user manuals,journal articles,quick reference guides and white papers. 

Data Scientist

A data scientist's job nature is generalized to multitasking as a mathematician to the product developer. A business requires big data to run smoothly and those big data are crunched into simple data for deriving the result. These results are mostly used to research their customer's preferences on their purchase behavior. These data are majorly collected, analyzed and shared with the IT and business teams of the company.

Data Mining

Data mining is gathering data for understanding the customer purchase behavioral data for forecasting intricate marketing strategies for a product or a brand. The customer’s movement will be monitored and analyzed for more data to understand the market trends and do an analysis with the market flow data. Study customer patterns on social media, purchase behavior, preferential online movement.

Machine Learning

Machine learning & AI are the system infused pre-defined algorithm responses that learn to respond on their own by making predictions. And they are not the usual coded responses that are done in generic software.

Database Administrator

The database is nothing but a compilation of data that is stored behind the software to run effectively. Database Analyst is the one who does the storage capacity allocation in the system for their projects. And License for the software they use for testing, developing and other processes are authorized to track by the DA. 

Full-stack Developer

In software development, there are two areas as front & back end that have technical data and UX/UI respectively. A full-stack developer's responsibility is to hybrid these two technical end under one hybrid working space for reasons like efficient functioning, deriving better results by avoiding communication lag between teams and to save time. They would be experts in programming languages like Python, Ruby, and similar types of software.

Web Programmer

Web developers/programmers are the designer and developers of www. Websites, they expertise in two areas that are back & front end. The back end is for the technical process and the front end is for the UI/UX to the site design. Their nature of work allows them to choose to do their job as a freelancer, or part/ full time in a company.

System Analyst

The system Analyst gets all the data from Business Analyst to do the technical work for the product. They should be strong in Programming languages, operation systems, computer hardware and string that knowledge together for product development. Identify and map the work flow chart in the technical end with respect to their client's needs. System Analyst is the one to give commands to software developers to get the job done.

Computer Programmers

All the apps that we use daily are developed and tested by a computer programmer. They design the workflow of app functions and rectify the coding errors & escalation. They can opt to work for a company or can do freelance work with flexible working hours. These coding programmers are employed in , IT, medical as well as almost in any other sector in this technological era.

Cyber security expert & Ethical Hacking

Every single company stores its company, as well as product data, in a server. Big companies like Google, Facebook employ ethical hackers for their company and give them the job to hack their server to check its possibility on breach level. And this will be monitored by the cyber security expert to do the analysis and find the areas that require security strengthening to prevent company data.

Software Consultant

Software Consultants are the ones acting as the link between the company and the client. They understand the client requirement and further do discussion. And then with that they collaborate with the development team and the client in the possible execution of the project. Collaborate with the client for a few days. Soft developers in future development and testing.

System Administrator

System admins are the ones responsible to set up the network for computers, from their technical end and then they do the server maintenance for mail and file servers. These experts possess in-depth knowledge of computer hardware, software, and networks.

Cloud Computing Specialist

Cloud is the expandable virtual space used to store a company or set of companies or an individual’s data. The benefit of the cloud is that it replaces physical storage space and also it is cost-effective. The cloud computing specialist makes sure the cloud data is available all the time. And they have strong working knowledge on system architectural components, virtual environment, and strong analytical skills.

Software Developer

The software application that you see on your phone and computer is designed, developed and tested by Software Developers. They are the ones behind the creation of OS like Windows, Apple, and Ubuntu. They analyze their client’s requirements and make the design accordingly. Software developers are the ones who give the user updates. They supervise and design the whole software.

Business Analyst

Software requirement is for almost everyone, and majorly people reach the software development company for a sophisticated end product for their business. And a business analyst in an IT sector collects the product expectation and requirement details from the client and shares it to the development team. Once the end product is ready the project presentation is done to the client. Business Analyst acts as the link between the client and the company in their product delivery.
