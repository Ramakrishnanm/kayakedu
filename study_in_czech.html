The Czech Republic or Czechia is a Central European country that is known for its picturesque cathedrals, hilly terrain, and a temperate climate. It’s a highly developed country and a social welfare state offering universal health-care and education to its citizens. It’s one of the most peaceful countries and offers an inexpensive cost of living to expatriates.

Over 44,000 international students have enrolled in the universities of Czech Republic, with more and more students pursuing higher education there every year. Well-esteemed universities, affordable tuition, colorful culture, and high yet inexpensive standard of living are some of the key attractive factors that pull foreign students
