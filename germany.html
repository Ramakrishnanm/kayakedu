Why study in Germany?

Germany is a beautiful country with a rich historical heritage, natural wonders and also with modern metropolis. German, which is the most spoken language all over the world. Its economy is the third-largest in the world.Some of the world’s renowned car manufacturing companies like Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, and Ford-Werke GmbH are German companies. There are some finest engineers in Germany who design and manufacture these automobiles, and there are some top engineering colleges which impart excellent education to engineers.

Germany has world-class technical, management, arts & medical institutions. The quality of education is among the best. It has approximately 450 universities with 17,500 programs.
There is no tuition fee at public universities. The tuition fee is funded by the state for domestic & international students. The mandatory sum to be deposited to blocked bank account is 8640 Euros ( about 7 Lakhs) which is less compared to other countries like US, Canada ( Rs.15 to 20 lakhs)






Work Rights 

Work opportunities are good if you know the German language.
You can work for 20 hrs per week (part-time) & earn up to 8 to 15 euros per hr & you can work for up to 120 full working days during vacations. No special permission is needed for work purpose. 

Stay Back

Foreign students get 18 months of work visa after studies & once you get the job & stayed in Germany for 3 years, you can apply for a permanent residence permit or apply for the EU Blue Card.
